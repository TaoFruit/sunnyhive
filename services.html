Course Overview

Who We Are
Sunny Hive is a pre-school for toddlers ranging between the age of 2-6years. We offer to learn through experiments and amusements, surrounded in a friendly atmosphere. Our motto is to bring out the best in a child and to build up their mental and emotional ability keeping in mind their physical vitality.
PROUD OF WHAT WE DO.
We are proud of being able to be a part of children’s development at the very beginning of their journey.
HOW WE WORK
We focus on the problems a child faces in order to communicate as well as their individuality is also of great concern.
WHY WE DO IT
At this crucial growth years, most of the children lack confidence, we try our best to inbuilt their communicative learning skills.
WHAT IS THE RESULT?
This results in the upliftment of a child’s mental health and he/she is able to bring out innovative ideas.
Who we are?
We are Sunny Hive, a pre-school offering the best your child can get.
What we do in academics?
Our academics include mathematics, science, arts all woven in an entertaining manner so that a child enjoys the learning.
How do we work for strategic plans?
We let children learn according to their own ability which further enhances their self-confidence and individual interest for learning.
Our overall action towards target?
Our target is self-improvement of every child day by day through interactive and playful courses.
The outcome result of awesome performance?
Children are able to cope up nicely in their primary schools. We promise to provide a sound knowledge of basics.
What is the purpose of Registration?
We ensure safety along with compatibility, each child is special to us. Special care is taken of all.

What do we teach?

1

Alternative Music

Our founder Mr. Bond believes that music is the best way to impart education through rhythm. Music is an essential part of our learning process.

2

European Music

Besides classical knowledge of music, western music is a part of our sessions which increases the child’s fluency.

3

Swimming Camp

Swimming is considered to be the best exercise. Physical fitness is equally our concern for a child’s growth years.

4

Classical Music

It helps kids to grab easy access to classic and traditional cultures. It helps in better understanding of their own tradition.

5

Asian Pop

Pop music includes various instruments which help the child to recognize and remember various instruments through the mode of entertainment.

6

Sing & Dance

Singing and dancing is an important part of our activity, we preach learning in this form of entertainment which kids find amusing.
